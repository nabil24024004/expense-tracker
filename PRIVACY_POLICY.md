# Privacy Policy for Expense Tracker

**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026

NeoSparkx ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Expense Tracker mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how our App collects, stores, uses, and discloses information.

You can read the online version of this Privacy Policy at:
[https://neosparkx.com/products/expense-tracker/privacy-policy](https://neosparkx.com/products/expense-tracker/privacy-policy)

## 1. 100% Offline and Device-Bound Data Storage
The App is designed strictly as an offline-first utility.
* **No Cloud Storage**: All transactions, accounts, budget goals, and debt/receivable logs are stored exclusively in a local SQLite database (using Room persistence) on your physical Android device.
* **No Remote Transmission**: Your financial logs, account balances, and transaction history are never uploaded to any remote servers, cloud infrastructure, or third-party databases.
* **Data Control**: You have absolute control over your data. If you delete the App or clear its storage in your Android settings, all stored data will be permanently deleted.

## 2. Permissions We Request and Why
To function correctly, the App requires the following device capabilities, which are handled purely on-device:
* **Biometric Sensor (`USE_BIOMETRIC`)**: Used strictly to secure your app access locally if you enable "Biometric Privacy" in Settings. We never read, store, or transmit your biometric templates.
* **Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`)**: Used to post local alarms and daily notifications reminding you to log your expenses.
* **System File Pickers**: Used when you request to export data (to Excel/PDF) or import backup logs. No generic broad storage access is requested.

## 3. Data Portability & Sharing
Since the App is entirely offline, we do not share, sell, distribute, or rent your personal data to any third parties. Any files exported (such as Excel sheets or PDF reports) are stored in directories you choose via the system file picker and remain under your complete control.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App does not collect or transmit any data from its users, including children under the age of 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be updated directly inside the App and posted on our website.
